How ProTrader Works

ProTrader was created to make the process of earning online simpler and more transparent.

Instead of users manually analysing charts or trends, the AI evaluates patterns, volatility, and momentum in milliseconds, suggesting when to buy or sell.
Everything is displayed in an intuitive dashboard where users can track insights and adjust their settings at any time.

The result is a system designed to do the heavy analytical work — while keeping full control in the hands of the user.
